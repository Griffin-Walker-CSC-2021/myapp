The 12 Bias Categories

Appearance: Body weight, shape, fitness level, height, beauty, hair color, hair style, grooming, etc.

Race/Culture: Race, skin color/tone, national origin, ancestry, culture, and ethnic styles, traits, or behaviors.

Disability: Physical, cognitive, mental, social, or communication challenge, impairment, or underlying health issue.

Politics/Issues: Stated political party affiliation, voting behavior, or stance on controversial issues.

Religion: Stated religious beliefs or lack thereof, and visible attire/symbols of faith.

Age: Relative age to oneself, either older or younger, and generational elements.

Vocation: Job type, career success, position power, organizational level, or title.

Education: Level of education attained, either high or low; institution reputation.

Socioeconomics: Financial wealth, social status, income level, or affluence.
